Cloudflare Down: Half the Internet Disappears

When Cloudflare goes down and half of your favorite sites vanish like they were snapped out of existence.

What this meme is about

This meme jokes about how many websites rely on Cloudflare for DNS, CDN, and security services. When Cloudflare experiences an outage, it can feel like half of the Internet suddenly stops working, similar to Thanos snapping away half the universe in Avengers: Infinity War.
